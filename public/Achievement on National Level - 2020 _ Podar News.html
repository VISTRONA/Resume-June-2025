Achievement on National Level - 2020

16 Feb 2022

PIS Amravati student Master Aneesh Pramod Padole of Std.VII secured 1st Position in "Young Scientist National Level Project Competition" organised on 27th January 2020 at Prof. Ram Meghe Institute of Technology and Research, Badnera. In this competition 4700 students took part and presented their project. Master Aneesh received Laptop, Trophy and Certificate in this National level competition as a First prize.
